    About This Chatbot

    PrivyBot is your private, local AI chatbot powered by Ollama and LangChain.
    All conversations are stored securely on your device — nothing is sent to external servers.

    You can optionally:

    • 🌐 Enable web search to fetch up-to-date information from the internet (via DuckDuckGo).
    • 📄 Upload a PDF and ask questions about its content — perfect for articles, papers, and manuals.
